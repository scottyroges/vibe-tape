# Shareable Layers

When sharing a vibe tape, there are multiple distinct layers worth sharing — not just the final playlist.

- **Seeds:** The songs the user picked as input. Shows taste and intent. Useful for "here's what I started with" sharing.
- **The Vibe:** The description/criteria Claude generated from the seeds. This is the most unique artifact — a distilled mood in words. Great for social sharing, shareable cards, conversation starters.
- **The Playlist:** The actual generated song list. The practical output people can listen to.

Each layer serves a different sharing purpose. Someone might want to share just the vibe description as a mood statement, or share the seeds as a challenge ("make a playlist from these"), or share the full playlist to listen together. Supporting all three gives users flexibility in how they present their taste.
