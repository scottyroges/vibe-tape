# Shared Seed Resonance

In group/collaborative vibe sessions (Tier 3), when two people independently pick the same song as a seed, it should matter.

- Huge weight increase on that song's influence over the vibe criteria — it becomes the anchor of the playlist
- Do something cool in the UI to celebrate the overlap — animation, highlight, special callout on the shareable card
- Could surface it as a "resonance" moment: "You and Sarah both picked Midnight City"

The overlap is a signal of genuine shared taste. Leaning into it makes the collaborative experience feel magical rather than just additive.
