# Sample Cap for Free Tier

Ingest all of a user's liked songs regardless of tier, but limit how many are sampled during playlist generation for free users.

- Free tier: ingest everything, but only pull from a random/smart sample of ~1000 songs when generating playlists
- Paid tier: full library is available for generation
- Upgrade pitch: "You have 4,200 songs but we're only using 1,000 — upgrade to unlock your full library"
- This is compelling because the user knows their niche deep cuts are in there but aren't being used yet

Combines well with the playlist generation cap — free users get limited generations AND a limited song pool. Two independent levers for conversion without ever making the user feel like they can't add music.
